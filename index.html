Who is Sunil Nanwani ?

Sunil Nanwani is an IB DP Mathematics teacher at both Higher Level and Standard Level at an international school in Singapore. He is an experienced IB examiner and moderator and has been part of the IB Educatinal Network since 2012. Sunil is also currently an IB Mathematics Online teacher with Pamoja Education and has been part of this new exciting blended learning program since 2015. Starting this August 2017, Sunil will take up a new position as the Head of Mathematics at another international school in Singapore.
